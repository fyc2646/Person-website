About Me

I'm a passionate optical/photonics engineer with extensive experience in design and simulation, including FDTD, FEM, and MoM, particularly applied to nanophotonic technologies such as silicon photonics. I have a proven track record of leadership in multi-functional teams and collaborations with external customers and collaborators to achieve performance targets. With over 50 peer-reviewed publications (1700+ citations as of Oct. 2024) in optics, photonics, and condensed matter physics, I bring deep expertise in photonic design.
